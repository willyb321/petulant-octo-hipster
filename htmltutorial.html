HTML Things/Tricks:


Make Internet Explorer 6 crash:

  <style>*{position:relative}</style><table><input></table>

Create an email in default mail client to specified email:


  <a href="mailto:your@email.com">Send us an email!</a>

Send us an email!

Meta tags to prevent search engine bots:


  <meta name="robots" content="noindex">

How to do comments in HTML:

  <!-- insertcommenthere -->

How to do ordered lists in HTML:


  <ol>
  <li>Get Food</li>
  <li>Get Pizza</li>
  <li>Get Chicken</li>
  <li>Get Milk</li>
  </ol>

Example:


  1. Get Food
  2. Get Pizza
  3. Get Chicken
  4. Get Milk
